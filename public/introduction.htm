Introduction

Welcome and Thank You for Participating!


The following questionnaire is part of a student research project hoping to contribute to research on vaccination behavior. This means that the data provided by you will be handled with confidentiality and not be used outside of scientific purposes.

The questionnaire is expected to take around 10 minutes to complete.

You will be asked for some demographic information and general opinions. The main part consists of rating a vaccination scenario. Please make sure that you read the scenario closely. We will also kindly ask you to briefly explain the reasoning behind your decisions.

Please note that:

  • You must be at least 18 years old to participate in this survey.
  • The decision to participate in this research project is voluntary. You do not have to participate and you can refuse to answer any question. Even if you begin the survey, you can stop at any time by closing your browser.
  • There are no foreseeable risks or discomforts to you for taking part in this study.
  • You will be compensated for good faith participation in this experiment with $-USD. Submitted tasks will be rejected if they are completed in a manner that appears to be random, incomplete, or otherwise negligent.

If you have understood this introduction and agree with its content, press the "Done" button at the top of the screen to start the questionnaire.
